# Naming Brainstorm — AAS (Auto Workshop + Dealership Mgmt)

## Abstract / Coinages
1. **Fleet** — short, punchy, evokes management & movement
2. **Velo** — from "velocity"; sleek, fast, two syllables
3. **Axle** — automotive core component, sturdy, memorable
4. **Nexar** — "nexus" + automotive; sounds premium
5. **Pivot** — turns, decisions, business agility
6. **Cortex** — intelligent core of the workshop
7. **Synq** — sync your workshop operations; modern spelling

## Speed / Precision
8. **Torque** — rotational force, automotive-native
9. **Gear** — simple, universal, metaphorical for getting into motion
10. **Shift** — gear shift, also "shift" as in transformation
11. **Pulse** — heartbeat of the workshop
12. **Align** — wheel alignment, also team/business alignment

## Workshop / Garage Concepts
13. **Bench** — mechanic's workbench, also "on the bench"
14. **Bay** — service bay, short and spatial
15. **Deck** — clean desk, clean operations
16. **Rack** — parts rack, roof rack, sturdy
17. **Rig** — workshop rig, also slang for setup

## African-Inspired
18. **Kazi** — Swahili: "work" — simple, universal, professional
19. **Doka** — Hausa: "workshop" / garage — authentic, distinctive
20. **Kere** — Yoruba: "to drive" / steering — short, action-oriented
21. **Raba** — Hausa: "to fix" / repair — solid, trustworthy
22. **Tender** — universal, but also Nigerian Pidgin English for "attention to detail"
23. **Gara** — Yoruba: "skilled craftsman" — expert positioning

## Compound / Two-Word
24. **BaySync** — service bay + synchronization
25. **AutoKazi** — auto + Swahili work
26. **ShiftWise** — gear shift + system wisdom
27. **GarajLogic** — garage + garage (Hausa: *garaj*) + logic
28. **TorquePro** — straightforward professional positioning
29. **WorkBay** — simple, descriptive, trustworthy
30. **DeckMech** — clean desk + mechanic
